Welcome to my Neighbourhood website!

This application helps you :

1 - Click on a location to find the reachability area based on a travel-time/distance and travel mode

2 - Find the facilities withing the reachability area with the associated bar chart of the distribution of the facilities

How it works :

In the reachability tab, select between travel-time (tme) and travel distance (dst) in the first row. In the second raw secect the travel mode (car, cycle, walk, wheelchair). Activate the draw (drw) button and click on a location on the map to get the reachability area. After the reachability area is computed, click on the facilities within reach button to get the Facailitiies.
